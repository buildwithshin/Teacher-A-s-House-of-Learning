Our Story

We are a dynamic education center dedicated to providing personalized learning solutions for students of all ages. Established in 2018 in Progressive Village, Brgy. Tagapo, City of Santa Rosa, Laguna, Teacher A’s House of Learning offers after-school programs, enrichment classes, playschool, and training and development services. Committed to inclusivity and excellence, we strive to be a beacon of non-discriminative education, fostering a nurturing and supportive environment where all students can reach their full potential through expert instruction and innovative teaching methods.

Our History

We started in a small garage at the City of Santa Rosa, Laguna, and as a result of the success and positive reception of our first branch, it paved the way for expansion. Our expansion began with the establishment of our second branch in Brgy. Tunasan, Muntinlupa City. From there, we are now growing to 30+ branches nationwide, whether in bustling cities or towns.

Ms. Alexandria Tating a.k.a Teacher A, COO

Our Services

Teacher A’s provides expert educational programs and professional training to boost student learning, academic success, and personal growth. Our services include quality tutoring, skills development, and tailored learning solutions that build confidence and lifelong achievement.

Academic
Tutoring

We offer this program for preschool to high school students and select college courses. It’s designed to boost academic excellence using tailored, effective learning approaches.

Playbased
Learning Programs

We offer a program for children aged 1.5 to 4.5 years, preparing them for school through a play-based approach that promotes learning in a fun and engaging environment.

Reading and
Writing Program

We offer a language program for preschool to grade school students, including international learners. It develops reading, cognitive, and listening skills in both English and Filipino for a balanced learning experience.

Speak Up
Mastery Program

The program develops articulation, phonological awareness, sentence structure, expressive and receptive language, social communication, and overall language skills.

Exceptional Star Explorers Program

An after-school enrichment program offering training for special education and undiagnosed learners needing extra support through SPED tutoring, behavior management, and ABA therapy.

Careers

  • Facilitate learning sessions for children with diverse abilities in a fun, inclusive, and structured environment.

  • Collaborate with co-facilitators and parents to support each child’s progress.
  • Prepare and manage learning materials and activities tailored to each student’s needs and abilities.
  • Monitor student behavior and provide guidance that fosters self-confidence, independence, and emotional resilience.

  • Excellent English communication skills, both written and verbal.
  • Willingness to undergo training and professional development.

  • Licensed professionals (LET passer or equivalent) are a plus.
